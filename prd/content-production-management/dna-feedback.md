# E10 · DNA Feedback

> Part of [Mindvalley Content Production & Management System](../content-production-management.md)

> Intelligence-layer capability **#3** (augments produce). **Phase 2.** Propose-only. This is the deferred meeting item: the system feeding an asset back against its DNA automatically.

## Purpose

A first-pass review before a human looks. Given an attached asset, its DNA standards, and a multimodal model, surface evaluative notes — "hook lands at 0:06; DNA requires ≤0:03", "aspect ratio matches 9×16 spec", "CTA card present." This is Frame.io's frame-accurate feedback made *evaluative*.

**Propose-commit handoff (`Context/MoreContext/CLAUDE.md` §7):** the checks land in a **gap-flags field** the editor sees and acts on **before** review. The AI never blocks or advances a transition; a human still approves. Cite which DNA standard each flag came from.
